About Me

Aspiring IS professional from Davao

I'm a results-driven BS Information Systems student at Davao del Norte State College, recognized as a consistent Dean's Lister across multiple semesters. With three consecutive years of government-backed employment through DOLE's SPES program, I bring practical administrative, data management, and coordination experience to everything I do.

Currently serving as NSTP Coordinator Assistant, I actively apply records management, Excel-based database systems, and logistical coordination skills daily. I'm also a published researcher — my paper on "Information System Technology Plan for Net Central Game Central Panabo" appeared in the International Journal of Scientific Research and Engineering Development (IJSRED).

Technology is most powerful when it serves real people. I'm committed to building information systems that are not just technically sound, but genuinely impactful for the communities and organizations they support.
